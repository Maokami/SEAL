\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represent an integer modulus of up to 61 bits. An instance of the \doxylink{classseal_1_1_modulus}{Modulus} class represents a non-\/negative integer modulus up to 61 bits. In particular, the encryption parameter plain\+\_\+modulus, and the primes in coeff\+\_\+modulus, are represented by instances of \doxylink{classseal_1_1_modulus}{Modulus}. The purpose of this class is to perform and store the pre-\/computation required by Barrett reduction.

\begin{DoxyParagraph}{Thread Safety}
In general, reading from \doxylink{classseal_1_1_modulus}{Modulus} is thread-\/safe as long as no other thread is concurrently mutating it.
\end{DoxyParagraph}
